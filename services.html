Why Choose Our Services?

At Ebrikkho, we offer a range of professional gardening services designed to meet your specific needs. Whether you're looking to create a beautiful indoor garden, transform your rooftop into a vegetable garden, or maintain a corporate office space, we have the expertise to help.

Our team of experienced horticulturists and garden designers work closely with you to understand your vision and create a plan that fits your space, budget, and lifestyle.

We also offer flexible subscription packages for ongoing maintenance and care, ensuring your plants thrive year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What's included in the setup packages?

Our setup packages include the plants, pots, soil, and professional installation. We also provide detailed care instructions and a plant health guarantee. The cost of plants and pots is not included in the setup fee.

How does the subscription service work?

Our subscription services provide regular maintenance and care for your plants. Our experts will visit your location on a weekly or monthly basis to check on your plants, perform necessary maintenance, and provide care tips. You can cancel or pause your subscription at any time.

Do you offer services outside of Dhaka?

Currently, we offer our services in Dhaka and surrounding areas. Please contact us to check if we can service your location.

What happens if a plant dies under your care?

We offer a plant health guarantee with all our packages. If a plant dies due to our negligence within the guarantee period, we will replace it at no additional cost.

How do I book a service?

You can book a service by clicking the "Book Now" button on any of our service packages, or by contacting us directly through our website or phone. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and create a customized plan for you.
